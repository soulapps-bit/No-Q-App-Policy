Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Q App respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Q App mobile application operated by Xendrio App Solutions.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Q App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • GPS or real-time location data
  • Payment or financial information

2. Information We Collect

To maintain app functionality and prevent abuse, Q App collects limited, non-personal data:

  • An anonymous device identifier (UUID) generated locally on your device
  • Queue range selections submitted by users
  • Selected public locations or branches
  • Timestamps related to queue updates

This data cannot be used to personally identify you.

3. Location Data

Q App does not use GPS, background location tracking, or precise geographic data. All locations are selected manually by users.

4. How Information Is Used

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Display approximate queue levels
  • Calculate majority-based queue information
  • Prevent spam and excessive updates

5. Third-Party Services

Q App uses Firebase Realtime Database to store queue-related information.

6. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Q App is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
